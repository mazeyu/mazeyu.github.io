I am a final-year PhD candidate in the Vision & Learning Lab at Princeton University, supervised by Prof. Jia Deng. I received my B.Eng. in Electronic Engineering from Tsinghua University in 2020. My current research focuses on multi-view 3D reconstruction and procedural data generation for 3D vision tasks. Currently seeking full-time positions in Computer Vision and Graphics (available 2026).
